About

About Cristiane

From Rio de Janeiro and graduated in Business in 2015, I arrived in Ireland in 2018 like any student seeking the opportunity for a better and more stable life. In the middle of the pandemic, I went after an extra income and, to occupy my free time, discovered the love of making nails. Today, after two and a half years, I opened my space, where, with much love, I deliver self-esteem, beauty, and power to the nails of the amazing women who accompany me.

Mission, vision and values

Our mission is to provide impeccable and personalized service through a team of highly skilled.
It is our vision to be a place of reference where everyone feels comfortable and welcome. Our goal is that every customer receives exceptional service from the moment they walk in, until the moment they leave.
Our values are ethics, safety, and quality in the delivery of our services, transparency in the results, excellence in every step, and constant training in search of the best.

Be a Nail Boss

With the goal of expanding all this love for this universe of nails, I decided to specialize not only to build nails, but also to train new qualified professionals in the area.
Small groups and also individual workshops.
